Hoisin Rice Bowl

Home

Description

This is a quick and delicious meal that can be a go to high protein lunch or dinner. As long as you have a rice cooker the only real cooking part of this recipe is the chicken. I would recommend experimenting and adding whatever you want to this that will fit the flavor profile. You could have this for lunch every single day.

Equipment Needed

  • None

Ingredients

  • A chicken breast cut into bite size pieces
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Bean sprouts
  • Kewpie mayo
  • Cooked white rice
  • Cooking oil of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook your chicken pieces in a pan with some oil on medium heat until they have some color but are not too tough. Try not to crowd the pan.
  2. Add a large spoonful of hoisin sauce and toss to coat everything. Bring to low-medium heat and toss occasionally until the sauce has caramelized a little bit.
  3. Put the chicken over a bowl of rice and add a fist full of bean sprouts next to it on top.
  4. Drizzle some kewpie mayo on top and either stir to combine everything or leave as is. Bone apple teeth.
